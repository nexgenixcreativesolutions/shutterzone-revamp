CURRENT SITE AUDIT

// After reviewing shutterzonemedia.com, the following issues were identified — impacting conversions, first impressions, and perceived value with high-end realtors.

Area Current Issue Impact Status
Visual Design Flat, template-style layout with stock illustrations. Feels generic relative to the quality of your work. Reduces perceived value and trust with high-end realtors Critical
Hero Section Two competing "Book in WI / Book in IL" buttons push the headline down and create layout confusion. Cluttered first impression, weakened CTA clarity Critical
Mobile Layout Navigation collapses awkwardly. Booking buttons stack vertically consuming too much screen estate. Poor UX on mobile where 60%+ of traffic originates Critical
Typography Default system fonts with no typographic hierarchy. Headings and body text lack contrast and rhythm. Reduces readability and professional feel Moderate
Services Section Cards cut off on the right side. Some titles appear without context and descriptions are too brief. Visitors miss key services; reduces upsell opportunity Critical
Social Proof Partner logos (KW, First Weber, RE/MAX, Shorewest) present but not visually emphasized. Credibility opportunity being missed Moderate
White Space Excessive empty space between sections creates a disjointed scrolling experience. Feels unfinished; visitors lose engagement mid-scroll Moderate
Color & Branding Teal/dark navy is a solid foundation but lacks a premium accent or consistent visual language. Brand identity feels inconsistent Moderate
Footer Contact details and office hours are scattered with inconsistent indentation and layout hierarchy. Key contact info is harder to find quickly Moderate
SEO Basics Meta descriptions and page titles appear generic. Alt text on images should be verified. Affects organic search visibility Moderate
